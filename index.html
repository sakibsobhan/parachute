About Us

Welcome to Parachute Engineering Works.

Parachute Engineering Works is an experienced tool, mold, and die shop in Bangladesh that designs and builds tools and dies in our state-of-the-art facility. Parachute Engineering Works continues to reinvest in the latest equipment and software. Therefore, our clients receive the lowest cost per part, highest quality, and on-time delivery. Our talented and experienced tool and die makers and machinists provide a variety of services. These services include die repair, replacement dies components, fixtures, and gages as well as manufactured assemblies. We also offer additional services including drilling, grinding, tapping, deburring, welding, assembly, and contract manufacturing.

Parachute Engineering Works is a trusted tool and die supplier for many different industries. These industries include LPG Cylinder, Bike, Table Fan, agriculture, automotive, electrical and industrial components, food and beverage, food packaging, power transmission, and satellite/communications.
